Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a classic Filipino stew made with rich peanut sauce, tender oxtail, and fresh vegetables. It is best served with bagoong.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • Eggplant
  • String beans
  • Banana heart
  • Water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil oxtail until tender (about 2–3 hours).
  2. Sauté garlic and onion in a pot.
  3. Add the boiled oxtail and some broth.
  4. Mix in peanut butter until sauce becomes thick.
  5. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  6. Serve hot with bagoong.
